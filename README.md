## `> infra --case-study`

> Real architectural decisions I made on government and enterprise projects, and why.

| Decision | Why | Result |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **ECS Fargate** over EC2 | Unpredictable traffic spikes during government announcements. Needed scale from 2 → 20 containers in under 60s without managing instance fleets. | Eliminated OOM crashes during peak periods |
| **CloudFront + S3** for static assets | Edge delivery reduces TTFB for users in regional offices outside the capital — sub-100ms vs 800ms+ origin round trips. | 80% reduction in frontend load time for regional offices |
| **RDS Aurora** over self-managed PostgreSQL | Government compliance requires point-in-time recovery, automated backups, and 35-day retention — non-negotiable. | Zero backup ops overhead · RPO of 5 minutes |
| **Offline-First with background sync** | Registration in rural Ethiopia happens in areas with 0–1 bar of signal. The app must work disconnected and reconcile when online. | Took civil registration from weeks to 24 hours |
| **AES-256-GCM field encryption** | Tenant data in a multi-landlord SaaS must be isolated at rest, not just at the access layer. | Compliance-grade data isolation at storage level |
